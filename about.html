PLATFORM CLASSES has been born out of the growing need for high quality coaching that could keep pace with the ever-changing global prospective in the field of education. We have started a journey, which will never end, journey which will take us to, to greater heights with each step. Making PLATFORM CLASSES’ progress towards it‘s rightful pace is not going to be a task of a single person or a small group of people. It can be done only with the active contribution of everyone- the management, the faculty, the administrative staff, the students, the parents and the well wishers.
